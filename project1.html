HCI Project 1: Friendly Campus for Students from Atlantis

Project Overview

This project explores how Atlantis exchange students can better integrate with HKUST students despite cultural, linguistic, and social barriers. We identified pain points and proposed solutions such as a multicultural week and structured opportunities for interaction:contentReference[oaicite:0]{index=0}.

Personal Reflection

Working on this project allowed me to understand the real struggles that international students face when adapting to new cultural and social environments. I realized that while technical and logistical barriers can often be solved with structured programs, the deeper challenge lies in social acceptance and building meaningful relationships. Personally, this project encouraged me to reflect on my own experiences as an exchange student and how small initiatives—like cultural exchange events—can create lasting impacts on inclusivity.
